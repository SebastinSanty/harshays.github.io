About

I'm a senior at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, studying Computer Science and Statistics. My academic interests span theoretical machine learning, statistics, and its applications to networks. Currently, I'm a research assistant working on network growth models and graph matching algorithms. Before my foray into research, I was a software engineering intern at Akuna Capital.

Research

Network Growth Models

We develop an interpretable growth model that jointly explains the emergence of multiple structural and content-based properties of attributed real-world networks. Advised by Hari Sundaram.

Graph Matching

We develop a spectral network alignment algorithm and theoretically analyze its performance on correlated Erdős–Rényi graphs. Advised by Sewoong Oh.
